Computer Science engineering and technology's bachelor's degree overview

URocket’s unique curriculum focuses on hands-on education with lots of lab work. Students also get practical work experiences through optional co-ops.

CSET majors learn to plan, construct, troubleshoot and manage hardware and software in sophisticated data networks. They also learn website design and development.

computer science engineering and technology bechelor's degree highlights

Optional co-ops

URocket’s co-op program sets us apart from other universities. Engineering students get a minimum of three semesters
of paid experience in the industry.

Hands-on learning

There’s a bit of theory, but our courses focus mainly on doing. URocket students learn how to code and write
programs, build databases and more

Cutting-edge labs

You'll have access to a variety of labs, including a Local Area Network (LAN) and Wide Area Network (WAN) lab for
network design and troubleshooting.

career opportunities

The staff at URocket’s Career Development Center is dedicated to helping students hone their professional skills for
interviews. Most of our students are job-ready by their sophomore years.
