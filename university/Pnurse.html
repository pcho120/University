There are two admission pathways to our competitive B.S.N. program:

Direct Admission Pathway.

You will be directly admitted to the B.S.N. program as a freshman. Admission requirements include:

  • 3.6 GPA and 23 ACT/1130 SAT or 3.8 GPA for test-optional applications
  • Must maintain a 3.5 GPA at University of Rockets to keep your guaranteed seat in the program

    • Pre-Nursing Pathway.

    Admission requirements include:

  • 2.75 GPA and 19 ACT/990 SAT or 3.0 GPA for test-optional applicant
